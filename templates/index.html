Who we are

WeCare Services is a community-minded provider committed to human rights and personalised care. We work closely with participants, families, and support coordinators to build safe, flexible supports.

Disability supports

Our services are designed around each participant's goals, with practical delivery across home, community, and daily routines.

Community Participation

Structured day supports, appointments, outings, and social connection activities.

Home and Living

Reliable home support with personal care, domestic assistance, and routine planning.

In-Home Aged Care

Compassionate in-home support for older adults, including personal care, wellbeing checks, and daily assistance.
